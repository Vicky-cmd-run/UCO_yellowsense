This script guides a 7-minute live demonstration for CIOs, CDOs, and Business Heads. It showcases how the platform connects field visits, relationship management, and head-office intelligence.

## 🎤 Speaker Script & Walkthrough

### Part 1: Executive command center (1 min)
*   **Presenter**: "Good morning everyone. Today we are pitching YellowSense Customer 360. In traditional banking, data latency is 12 to 18 hours. RMs operate blind. YellowSense unifies everything in real time. As a Head Office executive, I log in and immediately see our system-wide KPIs: total portfolio value, mobilization trends, and critical AI alerts."

### Part 2: Field Force Mobilization (1.5 min)
*   **Presenter**: "Let's step into the shoes of our field officer, Arjun Rao. He is on the ground in Chennai. He opens `/zrt`. He sees his daily priority visit list mapped out. Let's select Kumar Textiles. Arjun checks in, triggering geo-verification. He meets the client and completes a need assessment: they need working capital, expansion finance, and are complaining about their POS merchant terminal. Look at the screen—the moment Arjun saves this, our AI engine computes a 84% propensity for MSME Expansion Finance and a 87% lead conversion probability. Arjun submits a ₹25L lead."

### Part 3: Relationship Manager Hand-off (1.25 min)
*   **Presenter**: "Our smart routing system assigns the lead immediately to Priya Nair, our Chennai MSME specialist. Let's switch roles to Priya. When she logs in, she doesn't see a cluttered CRM. She sees her portfolio matrix, showing which clients to Grow, Retain, or Nurture. In her Priority Action Queue, the top item is Kumar Textiles—moved there instantly by the system."

### Part 4: Unified Customer 360 (1 min)
*   **Presenter**: "Priya opens the Kumar Textiles profile. This is the centerpiece of the platform. Under one single view, she sees accounts, recent interactions, visits, and the active lead. More importantly, the AI intelligence panel recommends she 'Resolve the open POS complaint, then schedule an expansion consultation.' AI is fully explainable: it tells her the score is high because turnover grew 31% and the field visit captured explicit expansion needs. She clicks 'Accept Recommendation'."

### Part 5: Meeting Intelligence & Compliance (1.25 min)
*   **Presenter**: "Priya holds a meeting. Rather than writing notes, she inputs the transcript, and with one click of 'Generate Meeting Intelligence', the system extracts key warehouse plans, funding needs, interest rate concerns, and follow-ups. Finally, let's look at `/audit`. Every single action we took—from the ZRT assessment, the RM recommendation acceptance, to lead conversion—is recorded in a tamper-proof audit trail, fully supporting India's DPDP Act regulations."
